Our Story

The Ancient Forest Botanical Gardens was founded in 2021, in Louisville, KY. It's goal is to inspire and encourage people to get outside and enjoy nature. We have several beautifully designed gardens, spanning over 2 miles. The gardens are free for the public to enjoy. In addition to our gardens, we offer a rental space for all of your business and personal needs. For more information on our rental space, fill out the rental inquiry form below.
